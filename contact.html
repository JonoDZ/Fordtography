About Me

Fordtography is the name for Fashion Photographer Kaye Ford. Kaye is lucky enough to work Front of House and Backstage at LCM and LFW for 2 years now, and hopefully many more to come! She has regularly been published within Notion Magazine's pages and has worked on big campaigns for designers. Her social media is always active and she is regularly updating her Facebook Page and Instagram.

She has had images exhibited within the Association of Photographer's gallery.

She is always looking at shooting new and exciting creative things, so if you would like Fordtography's help just drop her an e-mail, or check out her blog/Facebook/Twitter.
